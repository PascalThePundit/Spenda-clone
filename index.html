Frequently Asked Questions

What is Spenda?

Spenda is a super app that allows Africans to spend cryptocurrency effortlessly in their daily lives.

How does Spenda work?

Spenda converts your crypto to fiat currency in real-time, enabling you to make payments, transfers, and purchases instantly.

What can I do with Spenda?

You can pay bills, make bank transfers, and perform card transactions using your crypto as a means of deposit.

Is Spenda secure?

Yes, Spenda prioritizes top-tier security measures to protect your funds and personal information.

Is there a minimum or maximum deposit?

No, there is neither a minimum nor maximum deposit.

Can I deposit Naira?

No, you can only deposit supported digital assets which are automatically converted to Naira at the best market rate.
